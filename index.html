About

I've been writing software professionally for over 15 years, mostly enterprise stuff during the day. Selinger Apps is my side project — a place to build things that are actually fun to make.

When I'm not coding, I play the clarinet, the trumpet or any instruments I get my fingers on in local bands and spend time with my family in a small town near Freiburg in southern Germany.

Everything here is built with care. No dark patterns, no data harvesting, no subscription traps. Just useful tools and products that respect your time.
